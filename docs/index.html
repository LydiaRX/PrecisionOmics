About

Company Profile

PRECISION OMICS | GOVERNMENT OF PAKISTAN PROPOSAL

Precision Omics is a joint venture between LydiaRx, Premium Labs and Apha Digital Health, established to transform Pakistan’s Healthcare system by introducing precision medicine and multi-omics diagnostics technologies that analyze a patient’s unique genetic and molecular profile to deliver personalized diagnosis and treatment.

LydiaRx is a Swiss based venture studio building AI-Native, regulatory compliant by design solution that transform pharma, biotech, life sciences R&D. The LydiaRx leadership team have over 100 years of collective industry experience across a multi of Global organizations, focused on Genomics.

Premium Lab (Pakistan) Pvt Ltd is a leading healthcare provider with a history of delivering healthcare services for nine years. Accredited by the Islamabad health regulatory authority, The Company has earned the president of Pakistan award in 2022, Its vision is to empower better health through diagnostics insight, aiming to become the most reliable healthcare services provider in region.

Alpha Digital Health is global leader in health technology innovation enabling the future of healthcare through cutting edge research, advance digital infrastructure, and confidential high impact solution that balance national priorities with clinical excellence.
