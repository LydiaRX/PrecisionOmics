Global Success Stories

International Case Studies & Lessons Learned

The UK 100,000 Genomes Project

100,000 Genomes Sequenced

Established by Genomics England, this landmark initiative sequenced 100,000 whole genomes from NHS patients with rare diseases and common cancers. It successfully integrated genomic medicine into routine healthcare.

Case Study: Rare Disease Impact

THE CHALLENGE: Patients with undiagnosed rare diseases spent years navigating diagnostic odysseys.

THE RESULT: 25% of previously undiagnosed patients received definitive diagnoses through WGS.
